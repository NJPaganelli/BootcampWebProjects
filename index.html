Research & Design Synthesis Researched the history of the Kashmiri shawl as a fashion and art historical garment that shows, in its migration from India to Europe, the progression of orientalist imperialism in opposite direction. Project led to further research into how contemporary design uses multicultural inspiration, and whether a form of imperialism remains in contemporary fashion industry. Historical research continues to inform design work leading to refined translations of motifs and themes into contemporary collections.
